The 2024 Design Pay Asia (DPA) Report presents findings from a survey of 1,797 design professionals across five Southeast Asian markets: Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, and Thailand, conducted between October and November 2024.

93.9% of respondents are aged 35 or under. Indonesia accounts for 79.0% of the sample, followed by Singapore (9.7%), Malaysia (5.1%), Vietnam (2.9%), and Thailand (2.4%).

Across the sample, 52.5% of designers negotiate their compensation and 70.3% reported career advancement of some kind in the past year. Pay, work models, and perceptions of fairness vary by market: 70.1% of Singapore designers work hybrid, while regional markets report more mixed arrangements.

What follows moves market by market. A single regional number would flatten differences that matter to how design gets paid here.
