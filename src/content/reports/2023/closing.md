This was DPA's first survey. 323 respondents, mostly in Singapore, gave us a baseline: enough to find the 42% gender pay gap, not enough to say much about Malaysia, Indonesia, or Vietnam with confidence.

Those questions became the 2024 survey: 1,797 respondents across six markets. We are glad they led somewhere.

This report is where the conversation started.
