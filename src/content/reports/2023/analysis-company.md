*The following data covers Singapore respondents only (N=223).*

## Remote work: 2.7× premium

Fully remote roles offer the highest compensation premium: over 2.7× higher than in-office roles and 1.8× higher than hybrid. This reflects the access remote work provides to global employers with higher salary bands, as well as the tendency for remote roles to be concentrated at more senior levels.

## Company type and location

Non-local companies consistently pay 67.1% more than local ones. Large enterprises offer the highest median pay, followed by startups. Agencies and consultancies provide earlier career opportunities. Non-profits offer the lowest median salary across all company types.

There is a 25.5% jump in median pay between company sizes of 51-200 and 201-1,000 employees. Beyond that, differences are less significant. Larger design teams correlate with higher earnings potential: solo practitioners have the lowest median pay but require the least experience, while teams of 200-plus have the highest median salary and the most experienced designers.

## Industry

Banking and Capital Markets and Consumer Products are the top-paying sectors, compensating the experience levels required well. Life Sciences pays the highest median but has sparse data points. Government roles provide earlier career opportunities for less experienced designers. Automotive and Healthcare offer the lowest pay.
